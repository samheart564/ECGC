Introduction

This list compiles all my ship evaluations (Samvaluations), ever since I started doing them.


Notes

  • Mini-event ships (ex. Marseillaise) or ships unaffiliated with an Event (ex. Houston II) are put with the Major event closest to when their Mini-event was run for the first time.

  • If an Event / Collaboration ship has a unique Augment Module, the Augment Module is considered as part of their ship's evaluation and not put seperately in the Augment Section. For a full list of Unique Augment Modules, see the Augmentation wiki page.

  • Ships with a Retrofit are put with the Event their Retrofit came out in, NOT with their original event.

  • There are missing events between Mirror Involution and Rondo at Rainbow's End. These events will get added upon their 1st Rerun.

  • Any events that were added to War Archives before Ashen Simulacrum will get added at my earliest convinence (which means probably never).

  • MOST DESCRIPTIONS HAVEN'T BEEN UPDATED SINCE THAT SHIP'S EVENT. IF THERE ARE ANY MISTAKES/CONCERNS/ETC. CONTACT ME ON DISCORD SO I CAN ADDRESS THEM ASAP.

Ship Samvaluation

Laffey (Retrofit)

Laffey (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is very balanced between Main Gun, Torpedo, and skill barrages, and further augmented by her self-buffs. Her bulk is also fine due to her Augment module providing a self-heal. Overall, she is a great ship.

Prinz Eugen

Prinz Eugen is a tank-oriented CA with great stats and meh skills. Her bulk is very high compared to other tank CAs, but her damage is very very low compared to other tank CAs. Her Augment Module armor break is nice, but doesn't fix her abysmal damage output. Overall, she is a good ship, but is outclassed by other easily accessible options in most situations.

Belfast

Belfast is a damage-oriented CL with good stats and skills. Her personal damage is hindered by 1 MGM, but her self-buffs are enough to bring it up to acceptable levels. She also has a smokescreen, which can situationally be useful. She is ideally equipped with an HE CL gun to take full advantage of her self-buff. Overall, she is a good ship.

Arizona

Arizona is a support-oriented BB with meh stats and skills. Her personal damage is pretty underwhelming, and her healing is too RNG to support a full fleet, even with her Augment module. Collection only.

I-58

I-58 is a damage-oriented SS with average stats and great skills. Her personal damage is pretty good for an Elite SS due to her self-buffs. Her buff to other IJN SS is nice, but not needed for her viability. Overall, she is a good SS, especially for newer players, but worse than all the relevant SR SS options.

Jean Bart

Jean Bart is an off-flag damage-oriented BB with great stats and meh skills. Her Skill 2 self-buff is reliant on using a bad BB gun, and the Augment module doesn't fix it. She also has a preload, but only MGM+1 to compensate for it. Her Augment module barrage is also mediocre at best, but it is free damage. Overall, she is a good ship.

Glorious

Glorious is a damage-oriented CV with bad stats and skills. Her augment granted her a 20% cooldown reduction on her first airstrike, which mitigates her slow reload somewhat. Her self-damage is also pretty bad due to 7 planes and average efficiencies. Collection only.

Graf Zeppelin

Graf Zeppelin is a damage-oriented CV with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is pretty good, with her Augment module giving her a carpet-bomb style on-strike barrage, improving her clearing capabilities. She is ideally sortied with an KMS equipment to take full advantage of her self-buffs. Her damage-reduction support to other KMS ships is nice, but not necessary for her to be good. Overall, she is a great ship.

Ayanami (Retrofit)

Ayanami (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. Her Main Gun damage is really awful, but her Torpedo damage is among the best in the game, especially when combined with her self-buffs. She is ideally equipped with a fast-firing Main Gun to trigger her skill and All-out-Assault barrage faster for maximum uptime. Overall, she is a great ship, but susceptible to the deficiencies of Torpedo targetting on auto-battle.

Vampire

Vampire is a damage-oriented DD with bad stats and skills. The heal she provides is not worth the damage loss from using this ship over other options, even with the Augment module enhancing the heal. Collection only.

Karlsruhe (Retrofit)

Karlsruhe is a damage-oriented CL with bad stats and skills. Her personal damage is awful, even with her Augment Module, due to only 1 MGM and no good barrages. Collection only.

Montpelier

Montpelier is a damage-oriented CL with great stats and skills. She is a very balanced ship, with a great combination of damage, bulk, and AA granted by her skills and Augment Module. She also can buff other CL/CA/CB in the fleet from her Skill 1, however supporting other CL/CA/CB is not required for her to be good. She is ideally sortied with a High Performance Hydraulic Steering Gear for maximum bulk as well as her skill requirement. Overall, she is a great ship.

Illustrious

Illustrious is a support-oriented CV with good stats and skills. Her personal damage is good, with 8 planes and an on-strike rocket barrage. Her support is also good, granting a large shield based on her own max HP to the Vanguard fleet, and some minor healing along with it. Overall, she is a good ship.

U-557

U-557 is a damage-oriented SS with meh stats and skills. Her personal damage is meh, and her Augment Module trades damage potential for consistency compared to the Dagger. Collection only.

Le Téméraire

Le Téméraire is a damage-oriented DD with meh stats and skills. Her personal damage is meh due to an aimed barrage from her Skill 1, but even that isn't 100% guarenteed. Her self-buffs help, but don't bring her up to standard. Collection only.

Taihou

Taihou is a damage-oriented CV with great stats and skills. She has great personal damage due to her 8 plane loadout, and multiple on-strike barrages, with a 3rd one possible if RNG favors it. One of the barrages offers support to the rest of your Main Fleet as well. She is also very tanky due to possessing Heavy Armor as a CV. Overall, she is a great ship.

Hood

Hood is a flagship-oriented BB with good stats and amazing skills. Her barrage is very strong, and has a 100% proc rate, making her a great damage option. She also provides a Reload buff to all Main Fleet ships upon her barrage triggering. Overall, she is a great ship.

Albacore

Albacore is a damage-oriented SS with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is pretty good for an SR SS, and is further amplified by her DoT and barrages. Overall, she is a great ship; best utilized alongside Archerfish.

Z23 (Retrofit)

Z23 is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is great due to her stats and self-buffs, and her AoA requirement halved. Her bulk is decent, but nothing special. Overall, she is a great ship.

Wichita

Wichita is a damage-oriented CA with good stats and skills. Her personal damage is really high, especially for Elite/SR ships. However, her bulk is frail even with her Augment module. She also offers a FP buff to other CL/CA/CB in the fleet. Overall, she is a good ship.

Nagara

Nagara is a damage-oriented CL with bad stats and skills. Collection only.

Maya

Maya is an AA-oriented CA with good stats and skills. Her AA is very high due to an additional AA gun mount from her Augment, and a massive AA self-buff. Her personal damage is also decent now with double Torpedo launches from her Augment module skill upgrade. Her bulk is also good due to her Evasion and Evasion Rate buffs, and stats. Overall, she is a good ship.

Sims (Retrofit)

Sims (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented DD with meh stats and skills. Her damage is very meh, and her support, while good, is not worth using her for over other damage-oriented options. Collection only.

Javelin (Retrofit)

Javelin (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. Her bulk is pretty good, especially for a DD. Her damage is very good due to her Augment module barrage, and she has great mixed damage from her Main Guns and Torpedoes as well. Overall, she is a great ship.

Kagerou (Retrofit)

Kagerou is a support-oriented DD with good stats and skills. She has good Torpedo buffs for the Vanguard fleet, and her own Torpedo damage is pretty good as well. Overall, she is a good ship.

Minneapolis

Minneapolis is a mob-tank oriented CA with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is pretty good due to MGM+1 CA guns and her self-buffs when she sinks an enemy. She has average bulk with her base stats, but her Skill 2 further enhances her bulk (and the Vanguard fleet's bulk with her Augment module). Her Augment Module isn't that amazing, and is a sidegrade to the default Elite Augment modules. Overall, she is a great ship, but can be redundant with other easily accessible options.

Tirpitz

Tirpitz is an off-flag damage-oriented BB with meh stats and skills. Her personal damage is very lackluster, and is only brought up to good levels when paired with Bismarck (which isn't worth building your fleet around). Collection only.

Edinburgh

Edinburgh is a damage-oriented CL with bad stats and skills. Her Augment module is an improvement over the default Augment modules, but doesn't improve her enough to be viable. Collection only.

Émile Bertin (Retrofit)

Émile Bertin (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented CL with bad stats and skills. Her Augment module is an improvement over the default Augment modules, but doesn't improve her enough to be viable. Collection only.

Gneisenau is an off-flag damage-oriented BC with bad stats and skills. Her Augment module is an improvement over the default Augment modules, but doesn't improve her enough to be viable. Collection only.

Kawakaze

Her self-buffs are good, but only for the first 3 battles of the sortie. Her Torpedo shield is nice to have, but not that relevant against most stages. Overall, she is a good ship.

Z46

Z46 is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. Her personal damage is really good due to her self-buffs and AoA. She is ideally equipped with an AP main gun to take full advantage of her self-buffs. Overall, she is a great ship.

Saratoga (Retrofit)

Saratoga (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented CV with great stats and amazing skills. Her personal damage is very good due to great on-strike damage, her on-strike barrage with DoT, and a desynced 10s barrage that also does good damage. Her Vanguard Fleet support is nice, but not necessary for her to be good. Overall, she is a great ship.

L'Opiniâtre

L'Opiniâtre is a damage-oriented DD with bad stats and skills. Her Augment module is an improvement over the default Augment modules, but doesn't improve her enough to be viable. Collection only.

Mutsuki (Retrofit) is a damage-oriented DD with very bad stats and skills. Her Augment module is an improvement over the default Augment modules, but doesn't improve her enough to be viable. Collection only.

Prince of Wales is a off-flag support-oriented BB with good stats and great skills. Her skills buff both USS and HMS ships, as well as giving her great self-buffs when sortied with USS or HMS ships. Overall, she is a great ship.

Ship Samvaluation

Baltimore

Baltimore is a damage-oriented CA with great stats and skills. She modifies her ammo type, so she prefers guns with the highest raw damage. Can be sortied with or without USS carriers. Her personal damage is also good. Overall, she is a great ship.

Alabama

Alabama is a flagship-oriented BB with good stats and skills. Her barrage is good, and it is timed, allowing her to stagger her damage more easily. She is best used in a mobbing fleet due to her gaining more buffs as the sortie progresses. Overall, she is a good ship, but can be a sidegrade to more easily accessible options.

Cavalla is a damage-oriented SS with good stats and skills. She prefers to be paired with Albacore and/or Archerfish for best results. Her personal damage is also pretty good. Overall, she is a good SS.

Allen M. Sumner is a damage-oriented DD with great stats and skills. She has higher AA than most ships, but it is not AA-carry level. She has great damage due to her stats, efficiencies, and barrages, giving her a well-rounded kit suitable for any fleet. Overall, she is a great ship.

Birmingham

Birmingham is a damage-oriented CL with good stats and skills. She is relatively bulky for her hull type, and she has good damage to go along with it. Her Skill 1 even offers minor fleet support due to reducing flagship Burn duration. Overall, she is a good ship.

Stephen Potter

Stephen Potter is a support-oriented DD with meh stats and great skills. Her personal damage is pretty awful, but her skills more than make up for it. Her Skill 1 gives both other Vanguard ships a great buff, and her Skill 2 gives all CVs a Damage buff of their own. Overall, she is a good ship.

Bataan

Bataan is a damage-oriented CVL with good stats and skills. She has high efficiency planes, and a solid barrage, but only 6 total planes hinder her. Overall, she is a good ship, but is outclassed by easily accessible options. Gives +1 CV RLD Fleet Technology.

San Juan

Ship Samvaluation

Noshiro

Noshiro is a mob-tank oriented CL with solid stats and great skills. Her personal damage (Main gun + Torpedo + Barrage) is solid. Her blue skill improves her survivability, and her yellow skill provides one of the best torpedo buffs to your fleet. Overall, she is a great option, best used as a torpedo-buffer / mob-tank.

Shirayuki
