Early Ship Recommendations Guide

Introduction

Here are some good ships players can get extremely early on in Azur Lane!


Guild Shop

See also: Guild Shop

Notes:

  • You can refresh the Guild Shop using Guild Coins, DO NOT USE GEMS TO REFRESH SHOPS

  • Retrofit all ships EXCEPT Helena, whenever possible. Helena Retrofit is lower priority compared to other retrofits listed.

Notable Guild Shop Ships
Ship Location Description

Unicorn

Guild Shop - Elite Ship

Unicorn is the best healer in the game. Retrofit as soon as possible!

Helena

Guild Shop - Elite Ship

Helena is a decent damage-dealer early on, and her support capabilities are top-tier for bosses.

Chang Chun

Guild Shop - Elite Ship

Post-Retrofit: Chang Chun is an amazing damage dealer.
Requires 1600 to make her work, however.

Portland

Guild Shop - Rare Ship

Portland is a great tank for mid-late game. Retrofit as soon as possible!

Phoenix

Guild Shop - Rare Ship

Has good bulk, along with good damage.

Pennsylvania

Guild Shop - Rare Ship

She is your best easily-accessible BB.


Medal Shop

See also: Medal Shop

Notes:

  • The other ships not listed here are mainly for collection purposes, or are sidegrades to cheaper ships listed in this guide.

  • Get one of the below ships from Beginner Rewards, and the other from Medal Shop. Duplicates are fine since they save bulins!

Notable Medal Shop Ships
Ship Location Description

Enterprise

Medal Shop - CV

Amazing damage-oriented CV.

Hood

Medal Shop - BB

Great flagship-oriented BC, suitable for any content. Unique Augment is a great improvement over base Hood, but it is not necessary until much later in the game.


Core Data Shop

See also: Core Data Shop

Notable Core Data Shop Ships
Ship Location Description

Independence

Core Data Shop

Post-Retrofit: Amazing support-oriented CV with great damage.


Map Drops

Notes:

  • Only the earliest drop location is listed. Go to the ship's wiki page for multiple drop locations.

  • Retrofit all ships EXCEPT Yamashiro and Fusou, whenver possible.

Notable Map Drop Ships
Ship Location Description

Leander

1-4

Easily accessible CL with a great price-to-performance ratio.

London

Shropshire

2-1 (London) /
4-2 (Shropshire)

Post-Retrofit: Frail, but have great damage.
Really need retrofit, are bad without them.

Nevada

Oklahoma

2-2

Have an amazing price-to-performance ratio, especially early-mid game.

Nelson

2-2

Post-Retrofit: Has a great barrage and good main gun damage. Without retrofit, she is meh.

Ranger

3-1

Has an amazing price-to-performance ratio, especially early-mid game.

Langley

3-1

Has great AA for a carrier, as well as decent damage for the price.

Hiryuu

Souryuu

4-2

Great damage-oriented CVs, with the drawback that they both need to be in the same fleet

Saratoga

4-2

Amazing mobbing CV with great damage and consistent damage output compared to other CVs.
Really wants Unique Augment Module & Retrofit, is average without them.

Juneau

5-2

Post-Retrofit: Has amazing AA along with solid damage.
Really needs retrofit, is bad without it.

Ark Royal

6-3

Great support-oriented CV with great damage as well.

Yuudachi

6-4

Post-Retrofit: Has top tier damage from a vanguard ship.
Really needs retrofit, is bad without it.

Isuzu

7-3

Has amazing AA, enough to single-handedly take care of all your fleet's AA needs. However, she offers nothing outside of AA.
