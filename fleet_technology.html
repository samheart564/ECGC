Acquiring Tech Points

See also: Tech Points

Getting tech points for some factions (Eagle Union, Sakura Empire) are very easy. For others, its challenging. Below are the easiest ways you can get tech points for obscure factions.

Point Thresholds:

  • Tallinn is guaranteed after your 60th Khorovod of Dawn's Rime D3 clear.
