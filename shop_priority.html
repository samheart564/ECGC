Shop Priority Guide

Author(s): Riceist, Samheart564
Current Version: 1.1.1 | Last Updated: October 30th, 2023


Introduction

Goes over all the permanent shops in Azur Lane, and what to obtain from them.


Notes:

  • For the full shop list, see this wiki page.

  • To view Resource Income, see the Common Resource Guide.

  • All times listed are in Azur Lane EN Server time (UTC-7).

  • Never refresh a shop using . It is not worth it.

  • To view the wiki page for a specific item, click on its image.

  • If you already have enough of an item, feel free to skip it in subsequent months. There is no point in buying extra items you will never use.

  • Key:

    • Red Outline: - Highly recommended purchase, very general use.

    • Green Outline: - Really good in specific scenarios; niche; only good on a few ships.

  • Items are ordered from left to right, top to bottom. Depending on your situation, items marked with a Green outline could be higher or lower priority than listed.

  • The tables are scrollable horizontally!!

Monthly Shops

Core Data Shop

Information:

  • Currency:

  • Reset: Monthly (1st) at 00:00 (UTC-7).


Priority:

  • Click on a gear image to go to its wiki page.

  • Aim for a minimum of 3 pairs of Type 1 Armor Piercing Shells + Super Heavy Shells.

- - - - - - -> Lower Priority - - - - - - ->

- - - - - - -> Lower Priority - - - - - - ->

Best General Use for BB Damage (+Hit, +FP)

Type 1 AP Shell

(AKA White Shell)

(Core Data Shop)

Main Gun CRIT DMG +25%

One of the best general use BB auxes in the game, due to its combination of FP and HIT. Usually used alongside Super Heavy Shell, High Standard Fire-Control Radar, Admiralty Fire Control Table.

Best General Use for BB Damage (+FP)

Super Heavy Shell

(AKA Black Shell)

(Core Data Shop)

Main Gun CRIT Rate +8%

One of the best general use BB auxes in the game for damage. Usually used alongside Type 1 AP Shell, High Standard Fire-Control Radar, Admiralty Fire Control Table.

Top Tier CV (post-Retrofit)

Independence

(Core Data Shop)

Has an excellent barrage, plane loadout, efficiencies, and an amazing retrofit skill (Supporting Wings). Requires retrofit to be good. Only high priority if you lack good CVs.
You only need 1 copy of her.

Increases Level Cap from 120 -> 125

Cogntiive Array

(Cognitive Array Locations)

The higher your ship's level, the more damage they do and the less damage they take.
Hard to get outside of Core Data Shop.
Not to be confused with Cognitive Chips!

Increases Level Cap from 100 -> 125

Cogntiive Chip

(Cognitive Chip Locations)

The higher your ship's level, the more damage they do and the less damage they take.
Not to be confused with Cognitive Arrays!

For Guided-missile Destroyers (+DMG)

SY-1 Missile

(Core Data Shop, Joint Battle Mystery Investigation)

Best (and only) Missile equipment in the entire game.

Only usable on Guided-missile Destroyers (DDG).

For Guided-missile Destroyers (+DMG)

Seal of the Four Gods

(Core Data Shop)

If Equipped by an Anshan Class DD: +16% Main Gun DMG, improve All-out-Assault.

Has 50 extra HP over a Repair Toolkit, alongside all the buffs it gives to Anshan Class DDs. Only equippable by DDs!

For Submarines (+OXY)

Improved Snorkel

(Core Data Shop)

Gives a large quantity of OXY to Submarines, allowing them to stay underwater longer (and therefore fire more Torpedoes).

For Submarines (+Geometric Accuracy)

Pressure-Resistant Hull Design

(Core Data Shop)

+20% SPD, moves Submarines forward another 8 units when entering battle.

Moves Submarines closer to enemy ships, making it easier to target and fire Torpedoes at enemies. Some ships' skils move them forward already (ex. U-101), making this equipment redundant.

For CV Timing Adjustment

Homing Beacon

(Core Data Shop)

-4% Airstrike Load Time.

Has less AVI compared to a Steam Catapult, but provides timing adjustment that could be situationally useful.

For CV Timing Adjustment

Elite Aviation Maintenance Manjuu

(Core Data Shop)

+4% DMG, +4% Airstrike Load time.

Has less AVI compared to a Steam Catapult, but the increased DMG dealt can make up for the stat difference. Provides timing adjustment that could be situationally useful.

For Medium Armor CBs

VH Armor Plating

(Core Data Shop)

Heavy Armor: -6% AP DMG taken, -3% HE/Normal DMG Taken; Medium Armor: change Armor from Medium -> Heavy

Drastically increases bulk from enemy pellets, and decreases bulk from enemy planes/torpoedoes. Mainly used on Medium Armor CBs; not good on Heavy Armor ships.

For HMS BBs ONLY

6CRH AP Shell

(Core Data Shop)

When equipped on a Royal Navy ship, +30% Main Gun CRIT DMG.

Comparable to / very slightly better than the Type 1 Armor Piercing Shell on Royal Navy ships.

Great +FP Auxiliary (for Vanguard ships)

Intel Report - Arctic Stronghold

(Core Data Shop Ltd.)

-6% DMG Taken from Siren Enemies (does not stack).

Provides a good balance of FP and HP, and is the only good +FP aux for DDs.

Best +FP Auxiliary (for Cruisers)

FuMO 25

(Core Data Shop)

Provides a great +FP and +ACC stat boost in exchange for sacrificing survivability compared to other Auxiliary equipment
Only good on Vanguard ships; other auxes are better for Main Fleet.
Can only equip 1 per ship.

Best +TRP Auxiliary

Type 93 Pure Oxygen Torpedo

(Core Data Shop)

Provides a massive +TRP stat boost in exchange for sacrificing survivability compared to other Auxiliary equipment.
